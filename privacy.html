Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use ContentBot for TikTok, we collect:

  • Account Information: Your TikTok username and profile information (via OAuth)
  • Video Content: Videos you choose to upload through our platform
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Publish videos to your TikTok account as you request
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Communicate with you about your account

3. Data Storage

Videos uploaded through our platform are temporarily stored for processing and then deleted after publishing. We do not retain copies of your videos.

4. Third-Party Services

We use TikTok's API to publish content. Their privacy practices are governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Revoke our access to your TikTok account at any time
  • Request deletion of your data by contacting us

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
